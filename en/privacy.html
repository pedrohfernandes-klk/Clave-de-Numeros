1. Data controller

For any matter related to personal data, you can contact us at geral@clavedenumeros.pt.

2. What data we collect

This website only collects the personal data you voluntarily provide through the contact form:

  • Name;
  • Email address;
  • Type of activity (optional);
  • The content of the message you send us.

The website does not use its own tracking cookies or traffic-analysis tools. The contact form is processed by an external form-forwarding service, which acts as a processor of this data.

3. Purpose and legal basis

The data submitted through the form is used exclusively to reply to your contact request and to follow up on the communication you initiate with us. The legal basis for processing is your consent, given when submitting the form, and the pre-contractual steps taken at your request.

4. Retention period

The data is kept only for as long as necessary to reply to the request and, where a contractual relationship follows, for the legal retention periods applicable to accounting and consulting activity.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell or pass on your personal data to third parties. Data may be processed by technology providers strictly necessary for the operation of the website and the contact form, bound by confidentiality obligations, or disclosed to authorities where legally required.

6. Your rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to request access to, rectification of, erasure of, restriction of processing of, and portability of your data, as well as to withdraw consent at any time. To exercise these rights, write to us at geral@clavedenumeros.pt.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the supervisory authority — the Portuguese Data Protection Authority (CNPD).

7. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated to reflect legal changes or changes in how the website operates. The version in force is always the one published on this page, with the date of the last update indicated.
