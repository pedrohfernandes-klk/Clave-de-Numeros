02 · Services

Organise. Support. Decide.

We support companies, sole traders, freelancers and online businesses at different stages. The service is defined according to the legal framework, the operations carried out and the information each client needs.

02 Service sheets 09 areas

Explore the areas in which we can support your activity.

Each sheet explains the purpose of the service, who it is for and when it is usually relevant.

01

Certified Accounting

We organise, process and keep the company's accounting information up to date. The service may include document recording, account reconciliation, statutory filings and year-end work.

Who it is for
Companies and organisations that require regular certified accounting.
It may include
Bookkeeping, reconciliations, recurring obligations, year-end accounts and financial statements where included in the engagement.
When it is useful
At incorporation, when changing accountant or when the available information no longer supports the business.

Ask about accounting

02

Tax advisory

Tax advisory analyses the framework and consequences of a decision before it takes effect.

Who it is for
Businesses and professionals facing changes, new income sources or significant transactions.
It may include
Tax analysis, identification of obligations and risks, and technical clarification.
When it is useful
Before a relevant decision, rather than only after a problem has arisen.

Ask a tax question

03

Management consulting

Accounting information becomes more useful when it is interpreted. We help clients follow revenue, costs, cash flow and results.

Who it is for
Businesses that need to turn accounting information into management insight.
It may include
Reading results, comparing periods and organising management information.
When it is useful
During growth, reorganisation, hiring or investment.

Discuss management support

04

Tax compliance and obligations

We monitor recurring returns, deadlines and tax obligations and communicate the information required in good time.

Who it is for
Companies, sole traders and freelancers with recurring tax obligations.
It may include
VAT, withholding tax, corporate or personal income tax obligations and statutory communications.
When it is useful
Whenever the activity involves recurring filings and several deadlines.

Organise tax obligations

05

Payroll and HR administration

We process salaries and payslips and organise the administrative obligations associated with employees.

Who it is for
Businesses with employees or preparing to hire.
It may include
Payroll, payslips, remuneration statements and Social Security communications.
When it is useful
Before the first hire or as the team grows.

Ask about payroll

06

Sole traders, freelancers and IRS

We support registration or changes of activity, the applicable framework, income and expense organisation and personal income tax returns.

Who it is for
Sole traders, freelancers and liberal professionals.
It may include
Activity registration, tax framework, recurring obligations and personal income tax.
When it is useful
At the start of the activity or when income, regime or obligations change.

Discuss self-employment

07

Online businesses

We provide accounting and tax services adapted to platforms, marketplaces, digital payments, online sales and remote services.

Who it is for
Online shops, digital service providers and businesses using payment platforms.
It may include
Platform reports, reconciliation of sales, invoices and receipts, commissions, refunds and digital documentation.
When it is useful
When several platforms, currencies, subscriptions or payment methods are involved.

Explain how my online business works

08

Investment projects

The firm's history began with investment projects. Support may involve gathering information, defining assumptions and preparing financial information for feasibility analysis.

Who it is for
Micro and small companies preparing an investment decision.
It may include
Information gathering, financial structuring and preparation of supporting documents.
When it is useful
Before an investment, financing request or application.

Discuss an investment project

09

Asset and property administration

We organise documents and monitor administrative procedures associated with assets and property. This is administrative and documentary support, not full financial asset management.

Who it is for
Clients who need to organise property documentation or related administrative procedures.
It may include
Document organisation, verification and administrative follow-up.
When it is useful
When documentation is dispersed or a process requires organised information.

Ask about assets or property
