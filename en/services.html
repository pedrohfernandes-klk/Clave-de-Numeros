02 · Services

Accounting, tax advisory and management services.

Clave de Números provides accounting, tax advisory and management services to companies, sole traders, freelancers and online businesses. The support we provide is defined around each client's activity, legal framework and specific needs.

02 Service sheets 09 sheets

The sheets below explain, in context, the purpose of each service, the situations in which it may be needed, the elements usually involved and the outcome it aims to achieve. The content is informative; the specific framework is always analysed in light of each client's reality.

01

Certified Accounting

We organise, process and keep your company's accounting information up to date. The service includes recording documentation, reconciling accounts and monitoring statutory filings — allowing you not only to meet deadlines, but also to understand how income, expenses and results evolve throughout the year.

Who it is for
Companies, single-member companies, family businesses and growing ventures that need regular, organised, certified accounting services.
What it can include
Classification and bookkeeping, document reconciliation, periodic filings, year-end closing and the preparation of trial balances or financial statements where included in the service.
When it makes sense
When incorporating a company, when changing accountants, or when the information available no longer keeps up with the needs of the business.

Ask about this service

02

Tax advisory

Tax advisory lets you analyse the framework and implications of a decision before it takes effect. The work consists of clarifying obligations, risks and alternatives within the applicable legislation, so you can decide with information suited to your situation.

Who it is for
Companies and professionals facing changes of activity, new sources of income, hiring, growth or other decisions with tax impact.
What it can include
Analysis of the tax framework, identification of obligations and risks, clarification of technical questions and support in matters involving the Portuguese Tax Authority.
When it makes sense
Before a significant decision — not only once a problem or an unexpected obligation has already appeared.

Ask about this service

03

Management consulting

Accounting information becomes more useful when it is interpreted. Management consulting helps you follow the evolution of income and expenses, cost structure, cash flow and results, creating a clearer basis for day-to-day decisions.

Who it is for
Businesses that already have accounting information but need to organise and interpret it to follow their own progress.
What it can include
Reading of results and indicators, comparison between periods, organisation of management information and regular follow-up.
When it makes sense
In phases of growth, reorganisation, hiring, investment or change of business model.

Ask about this service

04

Tax compliance

We monitor returns, deadlines and periodic tax obligations, communicating in good time what is needed and when. The framework always depends on the activity, the regime and the transactions carried out.
