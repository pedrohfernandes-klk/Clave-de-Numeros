03 Quick answers 20 questions

No question matches your search. Try another term or ask us directly.

When should I contact an accountant?

Ideally before starting the activity, incorporating a company, issuing the first invoice or making a decision with tax consequences.

Does every company need a Certified Accountant?

Companies subject to organised accounting require a Certified Accountant to oversee the accounting and related obligations.

What is tax advisory?

It is the technical analysis of the tax framework and consequences of a decision, operation or change before it takes effect.

How should I choose an accounting firm?

Consider technical rigour, response times, clarity, experience with the activity and availability for ongoing support.

Which documents are normally needed?

Invoices, receipts, bank statements, expense documents, payroll information, contracts and other activity-related records.

How often should documents be sent?

The frequency should match the volume of documents and obligations. Regular delivery prevents backlogs.

What is Certified Accounting?

It is the organisation, processing and recording of accounting information under the responsibility of a Certified Accountant.

What does a trial balance show?

It shows account balances at a given time and helps monitor revenue, expenses, debts and available funds.

Are profit and cash available the same thing?

No. A business may be profitable but have limited cash because of unpaid sales, investments, payments or debt.

Simplified regime or organised accounting?

The answer depends on income, costs, obligations and expected development and should be assessed individually.

What does the Article 53 VAT exemption mean?

It is a VAT exemption for certain activities meeting the legal requirements in force. Thresholds and conditions must be checked at the time.

What is withholding tax?

It is an advance payment of tax deducted when certain income is paid.

What is needed to change accountant?

Documents, access credentials, responsibilities and accounting information must be transferred in an organised way.

What is the difference between Modelo 22 and IES?

Modelo 22 is the annual corporate income tax return. IES combines accounting, tax and statistical information.

What should be prepared before hiring an employee?

The information needed for admission, payroll and statutory communications should be gathered before the start date.

Does Clave de Números support online businesses?

Yes. Support may cover digital platforms, payment methods, online sales, remote services and documentation from several sources.

How should platform sales and receipts be organised?

Sales reports, invoices, fees, refunds and transfers received should be reconciled because the amounts may not match.

Is it worth asking for help with personal income tax?

It may be useful when the return includes rental income, capital gains, foreign income, inheritances or other complex matters.

How can the main tax obligations be anticipated?

With organised information, the main filing and payment deadlines can be identified in advance.

Do you work with clients outside Montijo?

Yes. Digital processes allow us to support clients in different parts of Portugal.
