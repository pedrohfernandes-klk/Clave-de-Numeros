C Frequent questions 02 answers
Company or sole trader: how does the support differ?

A company always requires Certified Accounting. A sole trader may be under the simplified regime or organised accounting, depending on the applicable situation. Support is defined according to the regime, the activity and the existing obligations.

Does a freelancer need an accountant?

It depends on the framework. Under the simplified regime it may not be mandatory, but it can help organise VAT, Social Security, income tax and other obligations. Under organised accounting, a Certified Accountant is required.
