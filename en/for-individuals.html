C Questions from individuals 03 answers
Is it worth getting help with my tax return?

When the return goes beyond routine — rental income, capital gains, foreign income, divorce or inheritance — professional support prevents errors and makes use of the legal options available. For a simple return, the cost may not be justified, and we will tell you so just as clearly.

I rent out a property. What do I need to declare?

Rents are property income (category F) and involve issuing electronic rent receipts, declaring the income in your tax return and, in some cases, taxation options worth analysing. Expenses related to the property can be deducted within the rules — as long as they are documented.

I sold a house. How do capital gains work?

The capital gain is calculated in the tax return from the sale value, the adjusted acquisition value and documented expenses. There are exclusions, such as reinvestment in a permanent home, which should be analysed before filing.
