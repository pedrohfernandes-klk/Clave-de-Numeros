C Questions from individuals 03 answers
Is it worth asking for support with my personal income tax return?

It may be useful when the return includes rental income, capital gains, foreign income, inheritances or other matters requiring additional analysis and documentation.

I rent out a property. What needs to be declared?

Rental income must be included in the personal income tax return. Documentation, eligible expenses and taxation options should be reviewed for the specific situation.

I sold a house. How is the capital gain calculated?

The gain is calculated from the sale value, the adjusted purchase value and documented expenses. Reinvestment conditions and other relevant circumstances should be analysed before filing.
