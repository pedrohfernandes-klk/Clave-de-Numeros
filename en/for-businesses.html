C Frequent questions 02 answers
What does accounting for a company include?

It can include regular Certified Accounting, tax and statutory filings, payroll processing where there are employees, and management information to follow the activity.

Do I need to be in Montijo to become a client?

No. Our office is in Montijo, but digital processes allow us to support clients anywhere in the country.
