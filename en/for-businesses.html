D Frequently asked questions 04 answers
Company or sole trader: how does the support differ?

A company always requires Certified Accounting; a sole trader may be under the simplified regime or organised accounting, depending on income volume. The support is adjusted to the regime, but your point of contact is always the same.

What does accounting for a company include?

Regular Certified Accounting, tax and statutory filings, payroll processing when there are employees, and management information to follow the business.

Does a freelancer need an accountant?

Under the simplified regime it is not mandatory, but it prevents errors with VAT, Social Security and personal income tax. Under organised accounting, a Certified Accountant is mandatory.

Do I need to be in Montijo to become a client?

No. Our office is in Montijo, but digital processes allow us to support companies and freelancers across Portugal with the same closeness and responsiveness.
