04 · About us

A firm that grew through its clients' trust.

The activity that gave rise to Clave de Números began in 2011. The company was incorporated in 2013 and provides accounting and consulting services to micro and small companies, sole traders, freelancers and online businesses.

A Our story since 2011

From investment projects to Certified Accounting services.

Accounting came about as a necessity. Isabel Monteiro's professional path began with the preparation of investment projects for micro and small businesses, including grant-funded projects and programmes linked to IAPMEI initiatives. The continued contact with clients after those projects were completed made it clear that a Certified Accounting service was needed.

The activity was initially carried out on a self-employed basis. In 2013, as the work grew and the structure needed consolidating, Clave de Números — Consultoria e Gestão Unipessoal Lda was incorporated.

The firm has grown steadily, largely by word of mouth. Some clients have been with us since the very start of their activity — a reflection of the trust built through professionalism, dedication and continuity of service.

Since its creation, Clave de Números has kept a strong family character and a particularly close relationship with micro and small companies. The way we work is personalised and people-oriented, never reducing our services to mere formalities.

Today, the team delivers accounting and consulting services through modern, digital processes fully compatible with remote support — without losing the closeness to each client. Fast responses, readily available information and rigorous work continue to distinguish the firm.

Professional ethics, rigour, responsibility, closeness and trust are the foundation of solid, lasting relationships.
