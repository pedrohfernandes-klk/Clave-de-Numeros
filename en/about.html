A Our history since 2011

From investment projects to complete accounting.

Clave de Números began in 2011 by preparing investment projects for businesses. Accounting developed naturally in response to clients’ needs. Since then, the practice has grown to include tax support, management consultancy, payroll and other areas, always centred on closeness, clarity and rigour.

Its growth has come through word of mouth, reflecting clients’ satisfaction with the work carried out.

B Origins of the approach management and communication

Understand, organise, follow through, clarify.

Clave de Números works from four principles: understanding each client’s activity; organising documents, deadlines and processes; following the accounting work consistently; and clarifying obligations and results so that clients can decide with confidence.

This approach grew naturally out of conversations with clients and an awareness of the critical points in the relationship between accountant and business.

C Principles in practice

Rigour, closeness, clarity.

These three principles guide every decision and the day-to-day work at Clave de Números.

01

Accounting and tax rigour

Every case is handled with technical responsibility, attention to documentation and respect for legal obligations. There is no room for improvisation or negligence.

02

Close client relationships

We know the reality of each client and maintain direct, accessible communication suited to their needs. We are not a distant department.

03

Clarity in communication

We explain procedures, obligations and results in plain language. Clients understand what is happening and why.

D Organisation how we work

A lean structure you can trust.

Clave de Números is a small practice of professionals experienced in accounting, taxation and management. Its scale allows for agile communication and a flexibility that larger structures cannot offer.

Each client knows who works on their account. There is no staff turnover or constant change of contact.

F What you can expect the working relationship

Consistent, predictable support.

When you work with Clave de Números, you know what to expect: clear communication, well-defined processes and a team that knows your business.

  • Availability: We respond promptly to urgent questions and maintain accessible communication.
  • Proactivity: We identify opportunities and risks in your tax and accounting position.
  • Organisation: We establish clear deadlines and processes, so you always know what needs to be done and when.
  • Transparency: We explain procedures, amounts and obligations. There are no surprises.
  • Flexibility: We adapt to changes in your activity. Processes develop as your needs evolve.
